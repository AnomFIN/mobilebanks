## Color Usage Summary

- **Blue Gradient Cards**: Balance card, summary cards
- **White Cards**: Content sections, forms, lists
- **Blue Buttons**: Primary actions (Vahvista maksu)
- **White Buttons**: Secondary actions (Quick presets, Share/Download)
- **Green Icons**: Credit transactions, success states
- **Gray Icons**: Debit transactions, inactive states
- **Soft Shadows**: All cards have subtle blue-tinted shadows
- **Rounded Corners**: 16px on cards, 999px on circular buttons
